Vibhaa Sivaraman

Member of Technical Staff,
Amazon AGI SF Lab

I work on browser agents and foundational models for the web in the Amazon AGI SF Lab. I was briefly at Adept AI Labs before our team moved over to Amazon. Prior to that, I graduated with a Ph.D. from MIT Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence Laboratory (CSAIL) advised by Professor Mohammad Alizadeh. My thesis focused on using computer vision techniques to improve video conferencing experience under low-bandwidth and lossy network conditions. I also worked on applying ideas from communication networks to payment channel networks to improve blockchain scalability, and network monitoring and heavy-hitter detection.

Previously, I received a B.S.E. in Computer Science from Princeton University where I worked with Professor Jennifer Rexford.

I am also trained in Bharatanatyam, a classical dance form from South India.
