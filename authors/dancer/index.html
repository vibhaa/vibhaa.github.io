I am a trained Bharatanatyam dancer for over 15 years. I began learning Bharatanatyam, a classical Indian dance form, from Guru K.P.Yesodha of Kalanjali, Chennai in 2003 and have continued since. My first solo performance was in Chennai, India in May 2011 and since then, I have given numerous solo performances both in India and in the US. I have been fortunate to have had many opportunities to dance with my dance teacher’s troupe also. I am always on the lookout for gigs and other performance opportunities in India and the US. Please contact me via email if you would like me to perform for you.
